Cliffs of Moher by Gurbe Douwstra (2005)
 published on his cd "Myn eigen paad".
For 8 years this song was #1 in the annual Frysian top 100
 and is world famous in Friesland.
The lyrics tells a story about love and a fatal accident.
Melody is Irish traditional folk.
Play slow and respectfull, in 3/4 beat.
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A7LW-3VqWcU

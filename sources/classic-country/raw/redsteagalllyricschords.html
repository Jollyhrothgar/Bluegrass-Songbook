Red Steagall lyrics and chords are intended for your personal use only. He has a wonderful voice and wrote and recorded many great country songs, many with a western theme.
 
Russell Steagall (Red) was born in the great state of Texas and a true Texan he is and so many of his songs reflect his love for it. When a teenager he loved riding bulls at rodeos and was good at it but then polio struck him at the age of fifteen. While in therapy he started playing the guitar and mandolin and the rest is history. He has done so much for country music and his music will live on for so many of us to enjoy.

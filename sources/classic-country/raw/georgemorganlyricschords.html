George Morgan lyrics and chords are intended for your personal use only. He had a wonderful voice and recorded many beautiful country songs that got him into the Country Music Hall Of Fame.
"Candy Kisses" was a number one hit on the Billboard country music chart for three weeks. He was the last person to sing on the stage of the famous Ryman Auditorium before the Grand Ole Opry moved to it's current location. Only a week after the move he was the first to sing on stage at the new venue. He had been a member of the Opry since 1948. It's still a treat to listen to his great music as well as play and sing it. He was a true icon in the good old country music.

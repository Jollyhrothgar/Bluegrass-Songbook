Ricky Nelson song lyrics and chords represents some of the best of the fifties music, maybe these lyrics and chords will remind you of yesteryear's great music.

He was born in 1940 and began his entertainment career at the age of 9 starting with radio and quickly moving into movies and singing. He was a very talented singer-songwriter, instrumentalist and actor.

Between 1957 and 1973 he had fifty three songs on the Billboard Hot 100. In 1987 Ricky was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. We will always enjoy the wonderful music he left us with.

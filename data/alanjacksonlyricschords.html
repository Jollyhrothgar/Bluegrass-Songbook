Alan Jackson lyrics are intended for your personal use only. He is a tremendous country singer and songwriter and began playing and singing music at an early age, mostly in church. He got his first job at the age of twelve singing in a local shoe store.

He formed his own band after high school and was soon on to a fabulous career. Alan's music is thoroughly enjoyed by so many and his awards vouch for that. He is a true talent and brings joy to those of us who love true country music.

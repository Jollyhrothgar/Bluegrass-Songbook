True country music is dying, play and sing these great old country and gospel songs lyrics with chords often to keep them alive. Bill Anderson is one of the most successful songwriters in country music history not to mention being a very popular singer. He grew up in Atlanta and studied journalism at the University of Atlanta working his way thorough as a radio DJ. During this time he tried his hand at songwriting and singing and it became a true passion.

He was only 19 years old when he penned "City Lights" and was recorded by Ray Price sometime later and quickly went to the top of the country charts. Bill moved to Nashville and landed a recording contract with Decca and what he has added to country music since then is incredible. He is a true icon.

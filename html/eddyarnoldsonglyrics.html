Eddy Arnold Song Lyrics

These Eddy Arnold song lyrics are just a few of his vast repertoire, but we hope you enjoy singing and playing them. Most of us country music lovers have certainly heard many of his beautiful songs, but I don’t think that any of us really know how many he recorded over his lustrous career. Enjoy using these Eddy Arnold song lyrics with chords to help you learn to play and sing a few of your favorites.

Eddy had 58 consecutive top ten country hits between his debut in 1945 and 1954 and never scored less than a top forty hit during the first 21 years of his career. He charted over a 140 records and sold a staggering total of 85 million of them and was dubbed as the Giant of American Music. 

These country classic song lyrics are the property of the respective artist, authors and labels, they are intended solely for educational purposes and private study only. The chords provided are my interpretation and their accuracy is not guaranteed.

Take time everyday to sing, even if it’s only for a few minutes, there’s no better stress reliever than music, now that’s just my opinion. Let’s keep these Eddy Arnold song lyrics alive by teaching some of them to your kids, grand kids and friends. Playing and singing together as a family can be such a pleasure.

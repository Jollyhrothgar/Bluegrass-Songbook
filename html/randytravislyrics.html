Randy Travis lyrics are intended for your personal use only, he's recorded many country and gospel hits. Randy has been very successful during his career with many of his recordings making the top ten.

He is considered a pivotal figure in the  history of country music and is known for his distinctive baritone vocals. He delivers his songs in a traditional style that has made him a true county music star since the late 1970's.
 
Randy has given us so many great songs to enjoy selling over 25 million records not to mention earning 22 number one hits and 6 number one albums. He has been awarded numerous awards over the years and was inducted into the Country Music Hall of Fame. He is a true legend in country music.

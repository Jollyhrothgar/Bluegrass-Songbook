His song lyrics bring back memories for those of us that lived in that era. Maybe these fifties, sixties and seventies lyrics with chords will add to your singing and playing pleasure. Although Elvis isn't considered country, he did record many great country songs. When us seniors were young, he was extraordinarily popular.

Elvis Presley, a cultural icon energized the entire world with his music which continues today. He began his career at Sun Records with the help of Sam Phillips who recognized a young talent with ambition. Elvis was one of the originators of rockabilly an up tempo mix of country and rhythm and blues.

A contract with RCA brought the single “Heartbreak Hotel” and was an immediate success with a number one hit. That was the beginning of a career that left us with so much wonderful music. Elvis Presley is regarded as one of the most important figures of 20th century popular culture and the best selling solo artist in the history of popular music. He had the versatile voice with such a wide range of sounds including country, pop, gospel, ballads and blues. What a talent!!!

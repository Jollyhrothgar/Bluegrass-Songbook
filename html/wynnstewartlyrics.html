Wynn Stewart lyrics represents many great country songs from the fifties till his death in 1985. Below are a few of his classic country lyrics and chords for your personal use only.

He did so much for American country music leaving many wonderful old songs. His voice was so unique and he had such a great way of delivering a song. He was very instrumental in the Bakersfield sound and such an inspiration in helping the great Merle Haggard and Buck Owens start their careers.

We all remember the wonderful old song "Waltz Of The Angles" which was his first hit. It will always be his signature song and one we will always enjoy. The biggest hit of Wynn's career was "It's Such A Pretty World Today" reaching number one on the country music charts staying there for two weeks. It was also song of the year in 1967 and won a gold record.

Those of us who still love great old country music know how much fun it is to listen to, play and sing Wynn Stewart's many recordings. Thanks Wynn Stewart for you contribution to country music.
